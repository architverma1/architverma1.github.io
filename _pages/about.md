I am a PhD candidate in Chemical and Biological Engineering at Princeton University. My work, supervised by Dr. Barbara Engelhardt (Department of Computer Science), focuses on developing and applying Bayesian machine learning models to uncover biological signals from a variety of single cell data. Before my PhD I was a Biomedical Engineering major at Duke University, where I worked on novel peptide synthesis for improved drug delivery with Dr. Ashutosh Chilkoti.
